The Solution

Agents That Remember

Agent Kit introduces persistent cross-session memory. Every fact, preference, decision, and past conversation is stored and recalled automatically — across sessions, across projects.

✓ No more repeating yourself between sessions
✓ Automatic extraction of patterns and preferences
✓ SQLite-backed Second Brain — searchable, persistent
